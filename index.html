The Course

The 2-week online course is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the journalistic process, from identifying newsworthy stories to conducting in-depth research, interviewing techniques, and ethical considerations. Through a series of interactive lectures, case studies, and practical assignments, participants will gain invaluable insights into the art of storytelling and the responsibilities that come with being a journalist.

Topics covered in the course include:

  • Understanding the ethics and basic principles of Journalism
  • Developing the investigative skills
  • Mastering the art of storytelling
  • Leveraging social media for journalism
  • Ethics in the age of digital media
  • Buidling your personal brand as a journalist
